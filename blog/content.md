### Content Creation

It's been four months since I first started thinking about it. It took me about a month to gather courage, and yesterday I finally did it.

Yesterday was the first day of me documenting my journey on Social Media.

For the longest time, I kept complaining: I don't have the right tools, I don't have time, location achi nhi he etc.

Turns out none of that was true, bas meri fat rahi thi, and instead of acting I was just procrastinating.

I didn't have the courage:

My top learnings from these routines:

- To face my reality,
- To stay authentic and
- To admit my flaws publicly.

But yesterday I just said, f**k it, and it happened.

I know that my lighting was too dark, facial expressions were bad and video was just too long but that's fine as long as I'm taking action.

I have been quietly working for the past 1 year without making any noise. I build, I break, I rebuild and I repeat.

I have no idea what I will be posting next but the first step is done, and that's what matters.

Honestly, this feels like an achievement.
